MISSION

Our mission is to help make the UVic student experience smoother, with a suite of free software tools.

ABOUT

VikeLabs is a team of UVic students who build apps that make the UVic student experience smoother. We're building a suite of free tools to help you succeed academically and extracurricularly.
We have two main goals, help students succeed at school and help students gain experience in the tech industry.

CURRENT PROJECTS

PASSR

For our first project we're building passr, a suite of tools for academic success which includes a grade calculator, an academic goal tracker, and a success planner.
The goal of Passr is to help students achieve the grades they want by spending less time organizing and more time studying.

JOIN US

VikeLabs is the only UVic club dedicated to giving Computer Science and Software Engineering students hands on experience in ideating, building, and launching web-based software products.
We accept members from every faculty who have an interest product design, software development, business, or product management.
If you're empathetic, scrappy, and want to make a difference for UVic students, then please join us!
